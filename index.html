About Me

Born in NYC and now residing in Pennsylvania, I have been coding since 2012. What started as an easy A class in high school with my best buddies soon turned into a career once I was introduced to Java deveopment in the 10th grade (after dragging my feet through Visual Basic). Since then, I've had the awesome opportunity to collaborate with students, teachers, and coworkers alike to make some pretty cool projects. You can check them out below!

I have had the opportunity to intern at The Nielsen Company in Tampa, FL in a DevOps role, and am currently working as a Software Quality Intern at Wegmans Food Markets. I also am a Software Developer for the startup company civ.works. Currently, I am a 4th year student at the Rochester Institute of Technology obtaining my 5-year B.S. in Computer Science (I live there when I'm not in PA!). For short-term goals, I am trying to expand my knowlege in DevOps and QA roles, as well as working to obtain my Salesforce certification for development! My dream job would have to be working for Blizzard Entertainment, as their games have allowed me to meet most of my closest friends today, and their games have been a big staple in my video game career growing up.

On a fun note, I am a HUGE gamer. Currently, my main games to play are Overwatch, WoW, various FPS games. I have mained a Windwalker Monk for 4 years in WoW, managed an all-time high of ~3300 SR in Overwatch, and am currently working on building my Twitch channel back up again.
